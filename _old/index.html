Predictors for Trickling in WSN

@lucaspetry, @doodomartins, @maikeps

Predictors on embedded systems for trickling in Wireless Sensor Networks (WSN). The objective was to decrease the number of messages exchanged by devices in an IoT environment, for instance, without losing important information. The project was developed during the Operating Systems II class, at UFSC.


TOC Compiler

@lucaspetry, @lucasmpalma, @luizurias1

A new programming language and its compiler, designed during the course of Compilers Construction. The language pillars are simplicity, organization and quickness. For the project we used Bison, Flex and C++.


Smail Simulator (Portuguese)

@lucaspetry, @nathanmolinari

A simulator of traffic to and from mail servers, developed using Javascript, HTML, CSS and the D3 library. Simulate mail traffic through different settings of servers and traffic distribution.


NBA 2014-15 Dataset Visualization

@lucaspetry

An interactive visualization of NBA movement data using processing. The project was developed during the course of Information Interfaces I took at UC Davis. It's possible to visualize play-by-play data, as well as the players and ball movements during an event.
